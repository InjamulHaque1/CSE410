How can I sign up for an account?

If you're a new user, click the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner to create an account.

How do I register my information?

Fill in the required details such as your name, email, and password. Then click the "Register" button to proceed.

How do I verify my email?

Check your email inbox for an OTP (One-Time Password) and use it to confirm your email address.

How do I enter the OTP?

Enter the OTP sent to your email to complete the email verification process.

How can I log in to my account?

Use your username and password to log in. Verify the reCAPTCHA and click the "Log In" button to access your account.

Where can I view my user profile?

Go to the "My Profile" section to view your account details, update information, or manage settings.

How can I edit or delete my profile?

Click "Edit Profile" to update your information. To delete your account, click the "Delete Account" button.

How do I browse products?

Navigate to the Products page to view a variety of items available for purchase.

How can I search for specific products?

Use the search bar to look for products by entering relevant keywords.

How do I add items to my cart?

Specify the desired quantity on the product page, then click the "Add to Cart" button.

How can I view my cart?

Click the "Go to Cart" icon to review items in your shopping cart before proceeding to checkout.

How do I complete the checkout process?

Go to the checkout page to review and confirm your order details.

Where can I find my order and payment history?

Access your order history and payment details in your profile. You can cancel appointments from here as well.

What information is available on the Appointment page?

Find details about available doctors and their schedules on the Appointment page.

How do I create an appointment?

Click the "Create Appointment" button and select a schedule based on the doctor's availability.

How do I fill out the appointment form?

Complete the form with necessary details and click the "Register" button to finalize the appointment.

Where can I view my appointment list?

View your appointment list in your profile. You can also cancel appointments here by clicking the "Cancel Appointment" button.

What is the Emergency page for?

Find blood availability across different hospitals by clicking on the Emergency option.
